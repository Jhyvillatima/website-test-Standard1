Description

This award-winning foundation is formulated with exclusive Micro-fil technology to sculpt and brighten the complexion, improving texture and blurring imperfections. Its silky texture glides on seamlessly, providing a flawless, luminous finish that lasts all day.

Ingredients

Water, Cyclopentasiloxane, Glycerin, Dimethicone, Titanium Dioxide, and more.

How to Use

Apply a small amount to the center of the face and blend outwards using fingertips, a brush, or a sponge. Build coverage as needed.
